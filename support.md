## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is ProfitPad?**
ProfitPad is a mobile app for tracking your expenses, income, and invoices — all stored locally on your device.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your financial data on our servers. See our [Privacy Policy](https://studio-7-dev.github.io/profitpad-legal/privacy) for full details.

---

### Free vs. Pro

**What are the free tier limits?**
Free users get:
- 1 Profit Pad (project)
- 50 Expenses per month
- 50 Income entries per month
- 50 Invoices per month

**What does Pro include?**
Pro unlocks unlimited Pads, entries, CSV/ZIP exports, PDF reports, receipt image backups, and all themes. Pro is a **one-time purchase** — no subscription.

**How do I upgrade to Pro?**
Tap the upgrade prompt inside the app or go to Settings > Upgrade to Pro.

---

### Purchases & Refunds

**How do I restore my Pro purchase?**
Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

**How do I request a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google directly:
- **Apple**: Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
- **Google Play**: Visit [play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory](https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory)

---

### Data & Backups

**How do I back up my data?**
Go to Settings > Backup & Export. You can export your data as a CSV file or a ZIP archive including receipt images (Pro only).

**Can I recover deleted records?**
No. Deleted records are permanently removed from your device. We recommend regular backups.

**How do I move my data to a new device?**
Export a ZIP backup from your old device, then import it on your new device via Settings > Restore from Backup.

---

### Troubleshooting

**The app is crashing — what should I do?**
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you are on the latest version of ProfitPad
4. If the issue persists, contact us at studio-7.dev@protonmail.com with your device model and OS version

**My Pro purchase isn't showing up.**
Try Settings > Restore Purchases. If the issue continues, email us at studio-7.dev@protonmail.com with your order confirmation.
